⚽ Chocotorta, the Argentine cake ⚽

Today we bring you the Argentine cake; known for the perfect combination of chocolate cookies, dulce de leche and cream cheese: the worldwide known chocotorta! The name chocotorta comes from the combination of chocolinas (the specific cookies needed to prepare it) and torta (which means cake). Probably one of the easiest and tastiest no-bake chocolate cakes to prepare... so simple, that it brings many men and boys to the kitchen to make it! The chocotorta is the most Argentine cake; and it is a super traditional dessert prepared for family parties and birthday celebrations. So easy to prepare and so delicious... just follow 3 steps and then enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 340 gr chocolinas (two packs)
  • 250 gr dulce de leche
  • 400 gr cream cheese or mascarpone
  • 100 ml coffee
  • 100 ml milk

Preparation

  • Combine the cream cheese and dulce de leche in a bowl; beat the egg until a homogeneous mixture is obtained
  • In another bowl, mix the coffee and milk to prepare the "bath" for the chocolinas. Tip: you can also try different variations of this "bisquit bath": just coffee, just milk, chocolate milk, syrup or liquor.
  • Dunk the biscuits in the coffee and milk and place them in a rectangular tray, until you have covered the entire surface
  • After you have completed the first layer, put a layer of dulce de leche mixture (of almost half cm. thickness)
  • Repeat the process, adding a layer of chocolinas and a layer of dulce de leche miyture until you have used all the ingredients.
  • Spread the last bit of dulce de leche over the last layer of chocolinas, and crumble some chocolinas over it to cover the cake for a nice finish
  • Leave it in the fridge for at least 2-3 hours.
